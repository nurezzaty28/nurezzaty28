Best Experience 2020
SHORT TRIP WITH FRIENDS



On the previous midsem break, I and with others friends decide to went to Rumah Ndoi Resort Baling to spend our breaktime to release our stress.
We went to there for 2days and 1night but for me it was very best moment or experience to me. After that, we also went to Baling Hotspring.
For us, when we stay in the hotspring water, all the stress will go away ;)
The next day, we woke up early to go breakfast the "Tiptop Corner" which is they sell "Roti Canai" in variety of choices. Customers can choose to add on cheese or not. The price is very cheap with that kind of taste. There are many outside people come to breakfast at this stall.


This is the best moment in 2020 with my friends because, all of us not come from Kedah and it more difficult to meet them because of this Covid-19 situation.
But all of us manage to spend a little quality time and still make sure we follow all the SOP to avoid spreading the Corona Virus.
Even it was the short trip but all of us still enjoying the moment. We only have a few months before this last semester end, so we manage to spend the time before we all split up and follow each other's choices whether to work or continue studying.
